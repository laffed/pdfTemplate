NFIP Advance Payment Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) Process

You have elected to receive your flood claim payment(s) via EFT directly into your bank account. In order to receive your claim payment(s) by EFT, you must have:


  • A valid active checking account with a U.S. based bank;
  • A valid active email account;
  • An active cell phone number that accepts text messaging and;
  • Access to the internet. (A gaming system such as Xbox, PlayStation, etc. will not work)

In order to receive your funds, you must successfully enroll in this program by following the below steps.

1.

After you have submitted a completed Advance Payment Request Form (below), you will receive an enrollment email with the Subject line "Selective Flood Claim Payment Information". This email will be from Selective Flood@vpayusa.com. The enrollment email will be sent to the email address provided in the Advance Payment Request Form within approximately 3 business days following Selective's review of documentation from the adjusting firm. This time frame may be expanded during a large flooding event. Remember to check your "junk" and/or "spam‚Äù folder for this email.

2.

Upon your receipt of the enrollment email, click on the link in the email to begin the enrollment process. You will be given 3 days from your receipt to successfully complete enrollment. If for any reason you are unable to complete the EFT enrollment process, the emailed link will become inactive after 3 days and your claim payment check will be mailed to the mailing address shown on your policy.

3.

After clicking the link, you will be required to create a password. Password creation instructions will be provided online. Next, to help verify identity, a text message containing a 6-digit code will be sent to the cell phone number provided on the Advance Payment Request Form. You must enter this code into the webpage within 5 minutes of receipt. If a valid code is not successfully entered within 3 attempts, your claim payment check will be mailed to the mailing address shown on your policy.

4.

After verifying your identity, you will be taken to the Terms & Conditions screen for review and acknowledgement. Next you will need to enter your bank routing and checking account numbers. You will have 3 attempts to enter a valid routing and account number. If you do not enter valid information after 3 attempts, the emailed link will become inactive and your claim payment check will be mailed to the mailing address shown on your policy.

5.

If the information is entered successfully, you will be taken to a confirmation screen and a confirmation email will be sent to you with a summary of the payment timing and details.

6.

If you have any questions about the EFT process or have not received the enrollment email, please contact our customer service team at (844) 343-3690. If you have questions specific to the handling of your flood claim, please call our claims team at (877) 348-0552.
